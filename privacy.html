Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Bible Garden is a free, open-source application for reading and listening to the Bible. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping things simple: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No accounts or registration required
  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising
  • No cookies

Server Logs

When the app communicates with our server to load Bible texts and audio, we temporarily log basic request information (IP address, requested endpoint, timestamp) for operational purposes such as monitoring server health and preventing abuse. These logs are automatically deleted after 14 days and are never shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Bible Garden does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app communicates only with our own server (api.bible.garden) to retrieve Bible content and audio files.

Open Source

Bible Garden is fully open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify these privacy practices yourself.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given the nature of our app, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out via Telegram or GitHub.
